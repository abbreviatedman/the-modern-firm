### Bot Afterschool Ideas

- Who we are in the inside
- Design a god
    - write down a profile of the god
        - name
        - domain (area of life they are in charge of or represent, as in "god of _____")
        - physical characteristics (hair color, gender, size)
        - symbols that represent them (like the moon for Artemis or the winged shoes of Hermes)
        - lineage
        - strengths
        - weaknesses
        - problems faced
    - design their look (draw it or dress up as them or represent them visually somehow)
    - make a poster about them
